Monero One is a privacy-focused Monero wallet designed for simplicity and security.

Features:
• Send and receive Monero with an intuitive multi-step wizard
• QR code scanning and generation for easy payments
• Real-time price charts with multiple fiat currency support
• Biometric authentication (fingerprint, face unlock)
• Background sync to keep your wallet up to date
• Home screen widgets for price and balance at a glance
• Light wallet mode for fast sync via trusted server
• Full node support for maximum privacy
• BIP39 and Polyseed mnemonic support
• PIN protection with PBKDF2 hashing and rate limiting

Monero One connects to public Monero nodes or your own node. Your keys never leave your device.
